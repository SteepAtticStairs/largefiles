In my experience, Free Download Manager has been the best for me for archive.org downloads. You can get it for free here. (I am not sponsored by Free Download Manager in any way.) The reason some seasons have YouTube playlists is also for the sake of speed. It is much quicker to watch / download videos from YouTube then from archive.org. It is command line based, but it is very quick and efficient. If you don't want to use the command line, you can find several websites that will download youtube videos for you as well.
